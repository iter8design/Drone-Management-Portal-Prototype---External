For flying drones weighing
250 g to 25 kg

You may fly:

  • In uncontrolled airspace with visual line-of-sight (VLOS):
    • at least 100 ft (30 m) horizontally away from people
    • at least 5.6 km (3 nautical miles) away from an airport
    • at least 1.9 km (1 nautical mile) away from a heliport

You may not fly:

  • In controlled airspace
  • Over advertised events (e.g. outdoor concert, sporting event)

Select this exam Advanced Exam
For flying drones weighing
250 g to 25 kg

You may fly:

  • In controlled and uncontrolled airspace with visual line-of-sight (VLOS):
    • over or near people within 100 ft (30 m) if flying a drone that meets the appropriate manufacturer RPAS safety assurance declaration
    • under 400 ft (122 m) above ground level
  • In uncontrolled airspace with visual line-of-sight (VLOS) using a visual observer
  • Sheltered operations around buildings and structures that might hide the drone from a pilot's view
    • horizontally near structures within 200 ft (60 m)
    • vertically above structures within 100 ft (30 m)
For flying drones weighing
25kg to 150kg

You may fly:

  • In controlled and uncontrolled airspace with visual line-of-sight (VLOS):
    • at least 500 ft (150 m) away from people
    • under 400 ft (122 m) above ground level
