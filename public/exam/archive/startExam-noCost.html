Before you start

There are 25 questions, each having 4 possible answers that must be completed within 60 minutes. For this test, the minimum pass mark is 60%.

If you close your browser at any time during the test, you may login again and resume your test within the allotted time. Should you run out of time, you may pay to complete the exam again.

System requirements

  • Javascript must be enabled
  • A wired internet connection is recommended. If using a wireless connection, you may encounter problems completing the exam if your wireless connection becomes unreliable.

How to navigate the exam

After answering each question, there is a Continue button to navigate to the next question, and a Go back button to navigate to the previous page. At the end of the exam, press the Finish button to complete the exam and get your exam results.
