In order to fly in controlled airspace or close to airports, an advanced pilot certificate is required. Without a pilot certificate, you are able to exercise your priviledges to fly a drone:

  • Outside 5.5 km of an airport
  • Outside 1.85 km of a heliport
  • Greater than 30m (98ft) horizontally of people
  • Outside of advertised events (i.e. outdoor concert, sports game, etc...)

Along with your pilot certificate, you must carry or be able to show this certificate when operating your drone as you may be asked for it by law enforcement agencies or a Transport Canada inspector.
