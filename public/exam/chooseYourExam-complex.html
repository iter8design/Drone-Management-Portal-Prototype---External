Advanced Exam $10.00

Select this exam Advanced Exam
For flying drones weighing
250 g to 25 kg

You may fly:

  • visual line-of-sight (VLOS) in controlled and uncontrolled airspace if flying a drone that meets the appropriate manufacturer RPAS safety assurance declaration:
    • over or near people within 100 ft (30 m)
    • under 400 ft (122 m) AGL
  • extended visual line-of-sight (VLOS) in uncontrolled airspace using a visual observer
  • sheltered operations around buildings and structures that might hide the drone from a pilot's view
    • horizontally near structures within 200 ft (60 m)
    • vertically above structures within 100 ft (30 m)
For flying drones weighing
25 kg to 150 kg

You may fly:

  • visual line-of-sight (VLOS) in controlled and uncontrolled airspace if flying a drone that meets the appropriate manufacturer RPAS safety assurance declaration:
    • at least 500 ft (150 m) away from people
    • under 400 ft (122 m) AGL

Complex (Level 1) Exam $50.00

Select this exam Complex (Level 1) Exam
For flying drones weighing
250 g to 150 kg

You may fly:

  • with all applicable rules for advanced operations
  • beyond visual line-of-sight (BVLOS)
    • in uncontrolled airspace for operations away from populated areas if flying a drone that meets the appropriate manufacturer RPAS safety assurance declaration
    • in uncontrolled airspace for operations over sparsely populated areas if flying a drone that meets the appropriate manufacturer RPAS safety assurance declaration and approved design plan
    • under 400 ft (122 m) AGL
  • visual line-of-sight (VLOS):
    • in controlled airspace over or near people (within 100 ft / (30 m ) if flying a drone that meets the appropriate manufacturer RPAS safety assurance declaration
    • in uncontrolled airspace at least 500 ft (150 m) away from people

Sheltered operations

The operation of a drone that is not in visual line-of-sight during which the aircraft remains at a distance of less than 200 feet (61 m) measured horizontally, from a building or structure and at an altitude no grater than 100 ft (30 m) above that building or structure.
