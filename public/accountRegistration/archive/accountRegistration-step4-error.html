Attention

  1. Please indicate if you are interested in becoming a flight reviewer.
  2. Because you indicated you were not interested in becoming a flight reviewer, you may not continue with account registration at this time.

Are you interested in becoming a flight reviewer?

We're looking for people like you to become flight reviewers when the regulations take effect.

Flight reviewers help ensure a safe operating environment for everyone. By applying to become a flight reviewer, you will have a direct impact on the industry and the next generation of drone pilots.

As a flight reviewer, you will do flight reviews with pilot candidates to verify their flying abilities against Transport Canada standards. When a flight review is successfully completed, the results will be submitted online through your account. You will be able charge a fee for this service.

Once your request is approved, your name and telephone number will be published on our web site so other pilot candidates can contact you to arrange flight reviews.

In the future, you will still need to meet prerequisites for being a flight reviewer, including:

  • taking and passing an exam
  • applying for a pilot certificate

We'll notify you when these services are made available online.
