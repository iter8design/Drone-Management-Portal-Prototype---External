{First name} {Last name} Apply to become a flight reviewer

Flight reviewers help to ensure a safe operating environment for everyone. By applying to become a flight reviewer, you will have a direct impact on the industry and the next generation of drone pilots.

As a flight reviewer who is affiliated with a recognized drone flight school, you will conduct flight reviews with pilot candidates to verify their flying abilities against Transport Canada standards. Once a flight review is successfully completed, you will submit the results through your online account. You will be able to charge a fee for this service.

There is a $125 non-refundable fee to apply to become a flight reviewer.

Eligibility

You must:

  • be 18 years of age or older
  • hold a Pilot Certificate - Advanced Operations and have held that certificate for at least 6 months unless waived by Transport Canada
  • have passed a Flight Reviewer Exam
  • have no enforcement actions against you from Transport Canada

What you need before you start

It will take approximately 3 minutes to apply.

  • A Pilot Certificate - Advanced Operations with a flight reviewer rating has already been issued to you and you may not apply again.
  • You passed the Flight Reviewer Exam on Friday, October 19, 2018
  • You will need a Visa®, Mastercard®, or American Express® credit card or an Interac® Online debit card offered by a participating financial institution. To use Interac® Online, your bank card will have an Interac® Online logo on the card. Interac® E-Transfer and Interac® Flash may not be used for online purchases.
