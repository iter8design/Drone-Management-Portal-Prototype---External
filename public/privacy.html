Privacy Notice

The Government of Canada is committed to protecting your privacy.

Owners of remotely piloted aircraft systems weighing between 250 grams and 25 kgs are required by law to register their aircraft systems. Operators of remotely piloted aircraft systems weighing between 250 grams and 25 kgs are required by law to have a pilot’s certificate if they are performing advanced operations.

In order to register a remotely piloted aircraft system, and/or to provide you with a pilot certificate, Transport Canada is required to collect information about you. This information is used for the issuance, administration, validation, monitoring, and enforcement of permissions under Canada’s new Regulations for Remotely Piloted Aircraft Systems. These regulations help ensure the safety of Canadians and Canadian airspace. Personal information may also be used, in aggregate, for research, program evaluation and policy purposes.

Personal information collected for registration and the issuance of pilot certificates is collected under the authority of the Aeronautics Act and the Canadian Aviation Regulations. Select information may be shared with Canadian law enforcement partners and other civil aviation authorities. Anonymous or aggregate information may be disclosed in keeping with the Government of Canada’s Directive on Open Government.

Personal information collected for registration and certification will be retained for a minimum of two years, in accordance with the Privacy Act, and for up to fifteen years following registration. Under the Privacy Act, you have the right to access and correct your personal information. Failure to provide mandatory information in the registration or certification process may prevent the registration of your aircraft system or preclude you from legally operating a remotely piloted aircraft system.

For more information about Transport Canada’s privacy practices in relation to the registration of remotely piloted aircraft systems and the certification of remotely piloted aircraft operators, please review Transport Canada’s personal information bank for Civil Aviation Personnel Licensing (PPU 005) in Info Source.
