Sign up for a business account: step 1

Attention

  1. Please enter the legal name of the business.
  2. Please enter your business email address.
  3. Please enter your business telephone number.
  4. Please enter your position title.
  5. The legal name of the business entered may not be greater than 150 characters.
  6. The operating name of the business entered may not be greater than 150 characters.
  7. The business number (BN) entered must be 9 digits long (the first 9 digits before the letters RT, RP, or RC).
  8. The business email address entered is not valid.
  9. The business email address entered may not be greater than 150 characters.
  10. The telephone number extension entered may not be more than 5 digits long.
  11. The telephone number extension entered must be numeric.
  12. The position title entered may not be greater than 50 characters.

A business account is needed in order to register drones for a business. A business account will allow you to:

  • manage and view all the drones for your business in one place
    • If you've already registered drones to your existing personal account, you will be able to transfer them at no cost to your business account and your drone certificates of registration will be updated accordingly.
  • share your business account with other staff
  • access your personal and business accounts using the same sign-in credentials you are using

Before we begin, we need some basic information about you and your business to get started. All fields marked with an asterisk (*) are mandatory. The information you provide is collected and stored in accordance with our privacy policy and is administered under the authority of the Privacy Act and other applicable laws.

This information may be disclosed to other individuals outside Transport Canada if you choose to invite others to access this account.
