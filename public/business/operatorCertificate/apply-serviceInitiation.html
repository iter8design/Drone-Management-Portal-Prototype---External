{Operating name} - {Legal name} (BN999999999) Apply for an RPAS operator certificate

An RPAS operator certificate is needed by anyeone who intends to fly beyond visual line-of-sight. The certificate serves to ensure that effective safety policies and procedures are in place for the organization and/or operations carried out.

There is a $250 non-refundable fee to apply for RPAS operator certificate.

Eligibility

Any person or organization who intend to conduct beyond visual line-of-sight (BVLOS) operations in Canada.

In addition to the operator certificate, drone pilots also require a Pilot Certificate - Advanced Operations or Level 1 Complex Operations.

What you need before you start

You will need to have identified:

  • an accountable executive
  • a drone operations and maintenance manual
  • a training program
  • standard operating procedures used during flight
  • processes to manage safety risks

Any supporting documents will not need to be submitted, but may be requested after issuance of the certificate.

It should take 3 minutes to apply for an operator certificate. To begin, you will need:

  • the name of the accountable executive
  • a declaration that you or your organization has a drone operations and maintenance manual, training program, and adequate supporting organizational structure in place
  • a Visa®, Mastercard®, or American Express® credit card, Apple Pay® or Google Pay®
    • Debit cards may be used if added to your Apple Wallet® app, or Google Pay® app.
