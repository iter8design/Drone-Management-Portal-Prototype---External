Responsibilities

You agree to:

  • maintain an adequate organizational structure
  • identify, assess, and mitigate risks
  • comply with training program requirements (as per CAR 901.219 (this link will open in a new window))
  • comply with maintenance requirements (as per CAR 901.217 (this link will open in a new window))
  • conduct operations safely (as per CAR 901.218 (this link will open in a new window))

Accountable executive

The accountable executive is the person responsible for the operations manual, training programs, and safety processes. This person may be contacted by Transport Canada to request any supporting documentation to ensure compliance with the responsibilities of this certificate.
