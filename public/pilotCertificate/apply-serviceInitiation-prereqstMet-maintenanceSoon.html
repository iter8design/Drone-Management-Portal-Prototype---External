{First name} {Last name} Apply for a Pilot Certificate - Advanced Operations

To conduct advanced operations, including flying in controlled airspace, near or over people, you will need a Pilot Certificate - Advanced Operations.

If you wish to fly over an advertised event, you need to apply for a Special Flight Operations Certificate.

Before applying, you must have passed an Advanced Exam, and have successfully completed a flight review. After completing your flight review, the Flight Reviewer will submit your results electronically. You will be notified when you may proceed with this application.

There is a $25 non-refundable fee to apply for a Pilot Certificate - Advanced Operations.

Eligibility

You must:

  • be 16 years of age or older
  • have passed the Advanced Exam
  • have passed a flight review performed by a drone flight reviewer to verify your flying abilities within the last 12 months

What you need before you start

  • You passed the exam on Friday, July 17, 2018. You're on your way!
  • Your requirement for a flight review is waived.
  • You will need a Visa®, Mastercard®, or American Express® credit card, Apple Pay® or Google Pay®. Debit cards may be used if added to your Apple Wallet® app, or Google Pay® app.
