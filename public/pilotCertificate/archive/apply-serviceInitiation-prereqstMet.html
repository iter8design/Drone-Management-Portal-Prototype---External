Apply for an advanced pilot certificate

In order to conduct complex operations, including flying in controlled airspace or close to airports, a pilot certificate is required.

Before applying, you must have passed a Small Advanced Exam, and you must visit a flight reviewer to perform a flight review and verify your flying abilities. On successful completion of the flight review, your results will be submitted electronically and you will be notified when you may proceed with the application.

There is a $50 non-refundable fee to apply for an advanced pilot certificate.

Eligibility

You must:

  • be 16 years of age or older
  • have passed a Small Advanced Exam
  • have passed a flight review performed by a flight reviewer to verify your flying abilities

What you need before you start

  • You successfully completed the exam on Friday, July 17, 2018. You're on your way!
  • We received the results of your flight review on Tuesday, July 28, 2018.
