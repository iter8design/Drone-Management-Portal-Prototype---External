Register a drone for a business

A business account is needed in order to register drones for a business. A business account will allow you to:

  • manage all the drones for your business in one place
    • If you've already registered drones to your existing personal account, you may transfer them at no cost to your business account and your drone certificates of registration will be updated accordingly.
  • share your business account with other staff

A business account can be setup from your account settings.
